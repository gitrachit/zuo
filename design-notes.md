# Design Notes (from approved prototype)

Direction: "ledger meets terminal" — machine-precise data, a human coach's voice.

## Tokens
- bg #0C1116 · surface #131A21 · surface2 #0F151B · line #1F2933
- text #E8EDF2 · muted #7C8894
- gain #45D0A1 (jade) · loss #E2685C (sienna) — NOT stoplight green/red
- ai/coach accent #E3C08D (parchment gold) — reserved for AI voice surfaces only

## Type
- Data/numbers: IBM Plex Mono
- UI: IBM Plex Sans (or Inter)
- AI voice (debrief, copilot narration headers): Fraunces serif — the signature.
  The serif-vs-mono contrast IS the brand: coach's handwriting in the ledger.

## Principles
- Clean over dense (TradeZella-grade clarity; avoid TraderSync clutter).
- Net-after-charges P&L is always the primary number; gross is secondary.
- Debrief card: parchment-gold border, serif body, one actionable line emphasized.
- Mobile: single column, session-first landing (today's debrief + equity + trades).
- Every AI-generated surface visibly cites engine numbers; no vibes-only claims.
- Copy tone: direct, honest, never preachy or shame-y. "The ledger says…" energy.
